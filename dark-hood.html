WHAT WE HAVE

We have the latest in streetwear, casual wear and some vintage items to give the edge to your look. If looking edgy and fashionable is on your list of must have's, then we are your go to store! We stock everything from leather jackets, boots, sneakers and so much more, so you can elevate your look with just one piece.

OUR STORY

We believe that achieving a unique look,that speaks to your your unique flavour should be accessible to everyone. Unable to find pieces that did just that, our founders, Henry Rozani and Samantha Kloppers, decided to create their own. The first items were produced in henry's mothers backyard. Today, even though we have grown to a national network of over 10 stores, we are still committed to creating, unique, outstanding pieces that do not compromise on quality or asthetics.
