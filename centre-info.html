Getting Here

Getting to us is as easy as pie!

Pine City Mall is located on Main Street Port Elizabeth, just off the N2 Freeway and is easily accessible by car, bus and taxi.

Address

Pine City Mall,174 Main Street, Port Elizabeth, South Africa

By Car

Pine City Mall Provides safe and secure free parking to visitors. Get Directions to us and your next shopping spree! HERE

By bus

Pine City Mall is accessible by the NMM Transport Service. A bus stop is located on Bankers Road, next to the taxi rank, Exit 5.

By Taxi

Pine City Mall has taxi-rank for those shoppers who need other forms transport. The taxi-rank is accessible from the back of the mall at Exit 5.

Support Services

Parents Room

Pine City Mall has Parent Room facilities for families.

Play area

A free soft play area is located next to the food court for parents to take their little ones along to enjoy.

First Aid

The Pine City Medical Centre is located to the right of the South Entrance onto Main Street. They have Doctors and Nurses who will be able to assist with any first aid requirements.

Wheel chair Hire

Pine City Mall has introduced a free wheelchair service for those with limited mobility.Contact or visit the helpdesk for more info

Leasing

Long Term and short term leases available
