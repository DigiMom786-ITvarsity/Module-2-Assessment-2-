WHAT WE HAVE

We have a range of the freshest fruits and vegetables, organic and pesticide free. Our range includes grains, freshly prepared salads and light meals and snacks to eat on the go. So whether you are shopping to prepare meals at home or just feel a bit pekish, stop by, we have what you need.

OUR STORY

Fresh Produce was founded in 2001 and today we still stand by the same principles of fair labour practises, being earth friendly and organic. All of our cocoa, vanilla or any other product we stock on our shelves have been certified organic and fair trade. So if eating healthy , while sustaining the planet and paying farmers fairly is important to you, put us on your shooping route.
