ALL ABOUT US!

Our Mission

We are a family orientated mall but also offer something for everyone. We pride ourselves on our retailers, who provide not only the best merchandise but also customer service excellence. To us health and safety come first but fun is a close second! We provide the space and you bring the vibe!

Current Attractions

We offer a soft play area for tiny tots, so you can keep them entertained between shopping sprees. Ample toilet facilities mean you never need to wait in line. We also host events throughout the month and have an arts and crafts market in our pavilion on the last Saturday of each month. Come And join the fun!
