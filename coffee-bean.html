WHAT WE HAVE

We have the best coffee, imported from around the world! We also stock the best teas and a variety of freshly baked goods. Come on over when you need a little "pick me up coffee" or feel the need to snack. We promise to tantalize your taste buds and keep you coming back for more!

OUR STORY

Our founder, Jabu Bhembe, has always had a taste for coffee, the more exotic and flavourful, the better. One day a favourite beverage turned into a business idea and the rest is history. So when the need to take a break arises, come in and explore the world of coffees on your doorstep!
