OUR STORY

Founded in 1998, Fashion Feet have been in the retail shoe industry for over 20 years. Finding a lack of retailers who privided comfortable, beautiful shoes to the market, Andrea Hodgekins decided to import shoes for herself. Soon friends were remarking on how beautiful and comfortable the shoes looked , that Andrea realised that other women were looking for the same thing and fashion Feet was born.Today we stock footwear from around the world and the pair you dont even know you were looking for!
