WHAT WE HAVE

We use only the best and freshest ingredients to create a culinary experience out of this world! We specialise in bringing flavours from around the world to you. Each week we have a spotlight on one ingredient, that we showcase in deliciously different ways from which they are normally experienced.

OUR STORY

The brain-child of a housewife, Melissa Garner, who became tired of cooking the same meals each week. Sharing a passion for cooking with her father, Deeno, the two met each week to try out new recipes. On her father's passing, Melissa missed those weekly experiences with her father and decided that she would open Deeno's Diner, in loving memory of the her father and their close bond with each other and food. If creating beautiful memories with your loved ones over a good plate of food is something you crave, visit us, we are sure to bring the magic!
